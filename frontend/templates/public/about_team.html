ABOUT SOL TEAM

SOL (Space Of Learning) is a specialized, competitive AI team driving technological innovation and securing top positions in major local and international hackathons. The team was founded with a clear vision to build advanced, AI-powered software solutions capable of competing on global stages.

In a short period, SOL has proven its excellence by achieving top ranks among hundreds of participants from various institutions, relying on deep research, highly efficient teamwork, and a relentless pursuit of engineering perfection.
