At SOL Data Engineering, we take the privacy and security of your data exceptionally seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the SOL Data Agent platform ("Service").

TL;DR: We collect only what's necessary to operate the platform. We never sell your data. Your uploaded datasets are processed in-memory and never stored permanently without your explicit consent.

1. Overview

SOL Data Agent is an AI-powered data cleaning platform. By using our Service, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. We encourage you to read it carefully and contact us if you have any questions.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Name and email address at registration
  • Hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords)
  • Optional profile details (job title, company)

2.2 Usage Data

  • Cleaning job metadata (file format, row count, strategy used)
  • API request logs (timestamps, endpoints, response codes)
  • Feature interaction events (pages visited, tools opened)

2.3 Uploaded Datasets

Files you upload to the Cleaning Studio are processed entirely in-memory for the duration of your session. They are not persistently stored on our servers unless you explicitly enable the "Save to Workspace" feature in a paid plan.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and manage your account session
  • To execute data cleaning operations and return results
  • To generate Audit Reports for your own review
  • To improve AI model accuracy and platform performance (anonymized only)
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, job completion alerts)

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Groq AI — AI inference provider for LLM calls. Only anonymized, contextual metadata (column types, missing rates) is sent — never raw data rows.
  • Cloud Infrastructure — Hosting providers (e.g., AWS) that process data under strict DPAs.
  • Legal Authorities — Only when required by law.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 30 days post-cancellation. Session datasets are purged from memory immediately upon session end or server restart, whichever comes first.

6. Security Measures

  • All communications are encrypted via TLS 1.3
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a work factor ≥ 12
  • API tokens are JWT-signed and expire after 7 days
  • Enterprise plans support on-premise deployments with zero data egress

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access a copy of all personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytical data collection

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@sol-data.io. We respond within 30 business days.

8. Cookies & Session Storage

We use a single first-party cookie (sol_auth_token) to maintain your authenticated session. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.
